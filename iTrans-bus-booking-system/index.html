WELCOME TO iTRANS

This is a bus booking website which helps passengers easily book, from one destination to another and in which you make all the payments via our website or through the phone.Once you have made the payment your ticket will be readily available.

Some few tips

In order to make bookings you will required to first sign up or login if you already have an account and fill in the details to the routes you will ply and make sure your wallet has the right amount in order for you to secure a seat.

If your accessing our site from a cyber, for security reasons you will be required to log out once your done booking. We guarantee our esteemed customers value in using our booking services.
